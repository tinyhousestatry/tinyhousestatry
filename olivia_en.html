Olívia

Tiny house for 2 - 3 persons.

Tiny House Olívia offers accommodation for 2-3 persons. It is built of wood. The interior is also dominated by wood. On the area of 18 m2 there is a living area with sofa bed (as a bed for 1 person), satellite TV and minibar. The fully equipped kitchen (fridge, electric cooker, microwave, kettle, kitchenware) is part of the living room. Bedroom part - 2 beds (located in the attic above the kitchen) has an area of 6 m2. The sleeping area is 2x1,80 m. You get there on the ladder. In the house behind the kitchen is a bathroom with shower, sink and toilet. In front of the house is a terrace, oriented southwest, equipped with garden furniture and parasol. The house has a parking space. For guests is available grill, charcoal, goulash-kettle, garden chairs, tourist maps, party games. Vah river with waterfront is only 30 meters. WiFi internet connection is obvious.
