Tiny houses Tatry

Your accommodation in Liptov region, Slovakia.

The small houses Tiny houses Tatry offers you stylish accommodtation in „tiny house“ style – eco friendly, new, wooden, modern. The houses are situated in Liptov region in the village Podturen - in detached part „Under blue mountain“. The towns of Liptovsky Hradok and Liptovsky Mikulas are only 5 km away. Liptov offers many opportunities for rest and relaxation in every season. Nearby are the Low, Western and High Tatras, Janska Valley, known for its thermal springs, ski resort Demanovska Valley Jasna – Chopok and Aquaparks Tatralandia and Besenova.
We offer accommodation in two detached small houses with full equipment for the best possible spending of your holiday.
