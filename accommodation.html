Accommodation in Tiny houses Tatry

We offer accommodation in two separate houses in the style of "tiny house".

Olívia

Lavender

Tiny Houses Tatry are located in the village Podtureň, in its new part "Undert Blue mountain" in Liptov. They are built on a fenced plot with an area of ​​610 m2. We offer accommodation in two separate houses built of wood, situated side by side on one plot. They are fully equipped to make your holiday best.

Each house has a separate entrance, lawn, private terrace and parking space. Together they offer accommodation for 6-8 persons. Wood also dominates in the interior. The area of ​​18 m2 and 25 m2 is available living room with sofa bed and satellite TV, dining area, fully equipped kitchen (fridge, electric cooker, microwave, kettle, toaster) is part of the living area.

The bedrooms are located in the attic and one bedroom on groundfloor. Each house has a bathroom with shower, sink and toilet. In front of the houses are terraces, oriented southwest. For guests are prepared bikes (1 trekking and 1 mountain), outdoor grill, charcoal, goulash kettle, garden chairs, tourist maps, party games. There is a WiFi internet connection.
